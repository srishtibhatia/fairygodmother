Ila Haldia

We transformed Ila, a working professional, by conducting:

  • - Wardrobe Evaluation
  • - Wardrobe Decluttering
  • - Shopping from own wardrobe
  • - Shopping list creation
  • - Grooming (skin care/makeup) tutorial
  • - Personal Shopping

Time Taken: 2 days

It gives us great pleasure to share with you our experience of dealing with one of the loveliest clients, Ila Haldia. She wanted us to ‘sort her wardrobe’ for her, but we ended up doing a little more than just that.

After the first session, this is what Ila had to say: “Like a lot of people, I am one of those who complain of having no clothes but need more than two wardrobes to put them in! Every occasion I sift through my wardrobe, but fail to find something nice to wear. After my image consulting session with Srishti, I had an arranged wardrobe of clothes, accessories and shoes that I loved but did not recognize that it existed; I felt more confident mix-matching clothes and layering them to uplift my look and have clothes for all occasions, and most importantly, I realized the importance of power dressing and how people react when one is dressed well. Srishti, despite being an old friend, was very professional throughout the session and her passion for this field reflected in her work. All in all, at the end of our first session I was happier, had an amazing wardrobe and was super excited to meet her for our next session!”

The next session was a Grooming and Make-Up Clinic where we explained the basics of skin care, make-up and grooming and gave a Make-up tutorial to her, for a daily wear and a special occasion look. Being a young lawyer at the Supreme Court, she needs to come across as authoritative, mature and reliable to her colleagues, who are much older gentlemen. Amongst other things that were advised to her, a routine of basic make-up was recommended. This gives a polished and professional look. After getting Ila dolled up, we went for her Personal Shopping. A shopping list had been created as a part of the Wardrobe Evaluation session. We spent a couple of hours at the malls and Ila was never more satisfied with a shopping trip!

(Please go through the pictures for a visual journey of her consultation.)

We wish Ila all the best for an exciting life ahead and her career as a lawyer!

  • Marks & Spencer, Ambience Mall

  • Wills Lifestyle, Ambience Mall 
  • Aldo, Ambience Mall 
  • Rosso Brunetto, Ambience Mall 
  • Brother’s Optician, Galleria Market

Favourite purchase: The Black and Ox-Blood Brogues from Aldo. The client has a classic sense of style and he prefers basics, but he understood that adding a statement piece can transform a dull outfit instantly. (These made the perfect pair for his wedding reception!)

Best Buy: Belted Jeans from Marks & Spencer.

Sharing the pictures with you all here. Do tell us what you think of our shopping haul!
